Discover Pakistan

I invite you to the journey to Pakistan - the world's fifth-most populous country, which has the world's second-largest Muslim population. We will spend most of our time in Lahore - the capital of Punjab province. By looking at pictures and videos you will have the opportunity to see the beauty of Pakistan.
